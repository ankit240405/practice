Characters

Walter White

Walter White is a high school chemistry teacher who, after being diagnosed with terminal lung cancer, turns to manufacturing methamphetamine to secure his family's financial future.

Saul Goodman

Saul Goodman is a flamboyant and morally flexible lawyer who specializes in defending criminals, often using unconventional and humorous tactics. Known for his catchy slogan, "Better Call Saul," he becomes a key ally to Walter White and Jesse Pinkman.

Hank Schrader

Hank Schrader is a dedicated DEA agent and the brother-in-law of Walter White, who is determined to dismantle the drug trade in Albuquerque. His relentless pursuit of justice often put him at odds with Walter, though he remains unaware of Walter's double life as a meth manufacturer.

Jesse Pinkman

Jesse Pinkman is a former student of Walter White and his reluctant partner in the methamphetamine business. Struggling with addiction and a troubled past, Jesse often grapples with the moral consequences of their actions and his own sense of guilt.

Gus Fring

Gus Fring is a highly intelligent and calculating drug lord who operates under the guise of a legitimate businessman, running a successful fast-food chain. Known for his calm demeanor and meticulous planning, he poses a significant threat to Walter White as he seeks to expand his drug empire.

Skyler White

Skyler White is Walter White's wife, who initially supports him during his cancer diagnosis but becomes increasingly entangled in his criminal activities as he delves deeper into the drug trade.She struggles with the moral implications of Walter's choices while trying to protect their family.
